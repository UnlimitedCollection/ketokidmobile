# 🧠 KETOGENIC DIET CALCULATION (COMPLETE GUIDE)

---

## 🔹 1. What is Keto Ratio?

Keto ratio = Fat : (Protein + Carbohydrate)

Examples:
- 2:1 → Fat is 2× (Protein + Carbs)
- 3:1 → Fat is 3× (Protein + Carbs)
- 4:1 → Fat is 4× (Protein + Carbs)

---

## 🔹 2. Energy Values

- Fat = 9 kcal/g
- Protein = 4 kcal/g
- Carbohydrate = 4 kcal/g

---

## 🔹 3. Core Formula

Energy per unit:
(R × 9) + (1 × 4)

Where:
R = keto ratio

---

## 🔹 4. General Steps

1. Calculate total calories
2. Calculate energy per unit
3. Find total dietary units:
   Units = Total Calories / Energy per unit
4. Protein:
   = 1 g/kg body weight
5. Carbohydrates:
   = Units − Protein
6. Fat:
   = Units × R

---

## 🔹 5. Example (22 kg child)

Total calories = 1860 kcal  
Protein = 1 g/kg = 22 g  

---

## 🔹 6. Calculations for Different Ratios

---

### ✅ 2:1 Ratio

Energy/unit = (2×9)+4 = 22  
Units = 1860 / 22 = 84.5  

- Protein = 22 g  
- Carbs = 84.5 − 22 = 62.5 g  
- Fat = 84.5 × 2 = 169 g  

---

### ✅ 2.5:1 Ratio

Energy/unit = (2.5×9)+4 = 26.5  
Units ≈ 1860 / 26.5 ≈ 70  

- Protein = 22 g  
- Carbs = 70 − 22 = 48 g  
- Fat = 70 × 2.5 = 175 g  

---

### ✅ 3:1 Ratio

Energy/unit = (3×9)+4 = 31  
Units = 1860 / 31 = 60  

- Protein = 22 g  
- Carbs = 60 − 22 = 38 g  
- Fat = 60 × 3 = 180 g  

---

### ✅ 3.5:1 Ratio

Energy/unit = (3.5×9)+4 = 35.5  
Units ≈ 1860 / 35.5 ≈ 52.4  

- Protein = 22 g  
- Carbs ≈ 52.4 − 22 = 30.4 g  
- Fat ≈ 52.4 × 3.5 = 183 g  

---

### ✅ 4:1 Ratio

Energy/unit = (4×9)+4 = 40  
Units = 1860 / 40 = 46.5  

- Protein = 22 g  
- Carbs = 46.5 − 22 = 24.5 g  
- Fat = 46.5 × 4 = 186 g  

---

## 🔹 8. Key Points (IMPORTANT)

- As ratio ↑ → Fat ↑
- Carbohydrates ↓
- Protein remains constant

---

## 🔹 9. Quick Exam Shortcut

1. Energy/unit = (R×9 + 4)
2. Units = Total kcal / Energy/unit
3. Protein = 1 g/kg
4. Carbs = Units − Protein
5. Fat = Units × R

---

## 🔹 10. One-Line Formula Memory Trick

Units = Total kcal / (9R + 4)

Then:
- Protein = 1 g/kg
- Carbs = Units − Protein
- Fat = Units × R

---

✅ This method works for ANY keto ratio (2:1, 2.5:1, 3:1, 3.5:1, 4:1, etc.)
