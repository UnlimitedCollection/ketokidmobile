# 🧠 COMPLETE KETOGENIC DIET CALCULATION SYSTEM

---

# 🔹 1. DEFINITIONS

W = Weight (kg)  
R = Keto Ratio (Fat : (Protein + Carbs))  
- Example: 2:1 → R = 2  
- Example: 3.5:1 → R = 3.5  

---

# 🔹 2. ENERGY VALUES

- Fat = 9 kcal/g  
- Protein = 4 kcal/g  
- Carbohydrate = 4 kcal/g  

---

# 🔹 3. CALORIE CALCULATION (PEDIATRIC)

For children:

- First 10 kg → 110 kcal/kg  
- Next 10 kg → 70 kcal/kg  
- Remaining → 30 kcal/kg  

---

## ✅ Formula

If W ≤ 10:
Calories = 110 × W  

If 10 < W ≤ 20:
Calories = (10 × 110) + ((W - 10) × 70)

If W > 20:
Calories = (10 × 110) + (10 × 70) + ((W - 20) × 30)

---

# 🔹 4. CORE KETO FORMULA

Energy per unit:
Energy_unit = (9 × R) + 4  

Total units:
Units = Calories / (9R + 4)

---

# 🔹 5. MACRONUTRIENTS

Protein:
Protein (g) = W  

Carbohydrates:
Carbs (g) = Units − W  

Fat:
Fat (g) = Units × R  

---

# 🔥 6. FINAL UNIVERSAL FORMULAS

Calories = based on weight rule  

Units = Calories / (9R + 4)  

Protein = W  

Carbs = (Calories / (9R + 4)) − W  

Fat = (Calories × R) / (9R + 4)

---

# 🔹 7. VERIFICATION FORMULA

Calories = (Fat × 9) + (Protein × 4) + (Carbs × 4)

---

# 🔹 8. EXAMPLE

W = 6 kg  
R = 3  

Calories = 6 × 110 = 660  

Units = 660 / (27 + 4) = 660 / 31 ≈ 21.3  

Protein = 6 g  

Carbs = 21.3 − 6 = 15.3 g  

Fat = 21.3 × 3 ≈ 64 g  

---

# 🔹 9. NORMAL RANGE (REFERENCE)

## 👶 Newborn
- Calories: 100–120 kcal/kg  
- Protein: 1.5–2.5 g/kg  
- Fat: 4–6 g/kg  
- Carbs: 10–14 g/kg  

## 🧒 Child (~5 yrs)
- Calories: 90–100 kcal/kg  
- Protein: 1–1.2 g/kg  
- Fat: 3–4 g/kg  
- Carbs: 8–12 g/kg  

---

# 🔹 10. UI DESIGN LIMITS (RECOMMENDED)

## Normal Diet
- Calories: 0 – 2200 kcal  
- Fat: 0 – 100 g  
- Protein: 0 – 50 g  
- Carbs: 0 – 300 g  

## Keto Mode
- Calories: 0 – 2200 kcal  
- Fat: 0 – 200 g  
- Protein: 0 – 50 g  
- Carbs: 0 – 80 g  

---

# 🔹 11. KEY RULES

- As R ↑ → Fat ↑  
- Carbs ↓  
- Protein = constant (1 g/kg)  
- Calories depend on weight  

---

# 🎯 FINAL SUMMARY

Input:
- Weight (W)
- Ratio (R)

Output:
- Calories
- Fat
- Protein
- Carbohydrates

👉 Works for ALL ratios:
2:1, 2.5:1, 3:1, 3.5:1, 4:1
